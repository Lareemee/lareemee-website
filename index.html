Before You Order

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

How do pre-orders work?

Our cinnamon rolls are baked fresh by pre-order only. Orders are reviewed after submission and confirmed once payment has been received.

When is payment required?

Full payment is required before your order is confirmed and added to our baking schedule.

Payments are securely processed through Square.

What is your cancellation/refund policy?

Because each order is freshly prepared and reserved for you, all confirmed orders are final once payment has been received.

Where is pickup located?

La Reemée is a home-based bakery. Pickup details will be provided after your order has been confirmed.

Allergy Information

Our cinnamon rolls contain wheat, dairy, and eggs. Some ingredients may be produced in facilities that process tree nuts, peanuts, soy, or other allergens.

Our products are prepared in a home kitchen and may come into contact with common food allergens.

Our baked goods are prepared in a home where pets are present. Pets are kept away from the kitchen during preparation, baking, and packaging. Work surfaces are cleaned and sanitized before orders are prepared.

However, we cannot guarantee that products are completely free from pet allergens. Customers with severe food or pet allergies should consider this information before ordering.
