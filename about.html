About Us

From a Love of Baking to a Dream in Progress

Hi, I'm the baker behind La Reemée.

What started as a simple love for baking quickly turned into something much more. As a full-time college student, I found myself spending my free time in the kitchen, experimenting with recipes, testing new flavors, and sharing homemade treats with family and friends. Before long, baking became more than a hobby—it became something I genuinely looked forward to every day.

La Reemée was created from that passion.

Every cinnamon roll is made in small batches and baked with care, from the dough to the finishing touches. My goal is simple: create cinnamon rolls that feel homemade, comforting, and worth coming back for.

Whether it's a classic flavor or a creative new twist, I believe the best desserts are made with quality ingredients, attention to detail, and a lot of heart.

Thank you for supporting my small business and allowing me to share something I truly love with you. Every order means more than you know, and I'm excited to continue growing La Reemée one batch at a time.
