About Us

From a Love of Baking to a Dream in Progress

Hi, and welcome to La Reemée.

I'm the baker behind every cinnamon roll, and together with my husband, we're building a dream that's close to our hearts.

What started as a simple love for baking slowly grew into something much more. As a full-time college student, I found myself spending every free moment in the kitchen trying new recipes, testing flavors, and sharing homemade treats with family and friends. Before long, baking became more than just a hobby. It became the part of my day I looked forward to most.

That passion became La Reemée.

Today, La Reemée is a small husband-and-wife business built with love, patience, and a shared dream. While I'm the one creating each recipe and baking every batch from scratch, my husband is by my side helping prepare orders, package every box with care, and grow this little business together. Every milestone, every late night, and every order is something we celebrate as a team.

We believe the best desserts aren't just made with quality ingredients, they're made with heart.

Every cinnamon roll is handcrafted in small batches, from mixing the dough to the final swirl of frosting. We believe homemade should feel special, comforting, and made with the same care we'd give to something prepared for our own family.

Our hope is that when you open one of our boxes, you don't just enjoy a cinnamon roll, you experience the warmth, comfort, and love that inspired us to begin this journey.

Thank you for supporting our small business and allowing us to share a piece of our story with you. Every order means more than you know, and we're incredibly grateful to have you as part of the La Reemée family. We can't wait to continue growing, one batch at a time.

With love,
The La Reemée Family
